# FlyRank AI Marketing Internship Kickoff Session

## Session Overview

The session was hosted by Adel, Director of Growth at FlyRank. The internship is a 6-to-10-week, self-paced program focused on practical experience in AI-driven marketing, business development, SEO, content strategy, and organic growth.

## FlyRank's Mission

FlyRank aims to build an AI-native company that helps people achieve significantly higher productivity. The team uses AI tools such as Claude, ChatGPT, and other models to improve marketing workflows, content creation, research, and search visibility.

## Core Topics Covered

### 1. AI-Driven Marketing
AI should be used to improve research, content production, strategy development, and decision-making. Interns are encouraged to test different AI tools rather than depending on only one model.

### 2. Copywriting
AI can support copywriting, but strong copy should still be based on customer psychology, clear messaging, and established advertising principles.

### 3. SEO, AIO, and AEO
Marketing is expanding beyond traditional Google SEO. Brands should also consider how they can appear in recommendations and answers generated by platforms such as ChatGPT and Perplexity.

### 4. Content Strategy
Consistent content creation and content repurposing are important. One useful idea can be adapted into posts, videos, articles, newsletters, and other formats.

### 5. Paid Ads and Funnels
Advertising should be connected to clear user intent, strong offers, and a well-designed conversion funnel.

## Internship Structure

The program is self-paced, optional, and community-driven. Interns are expected to use the portal, complete practical assignments, watch recordings, follow updates, and collaborate with other participants.

## Guest Mentors

The program will include guest experts such as SEO Steve and LinkedIn creator Yasmin Ali.

## Key Lessons

- AI should support human judgment, not replace it.
- Marketing recommendations should be verified with real evidence.
- A useful strategy needs a small and measurable test plan.
- Consistency and execution are more important than simply choosing a popular channel.
- Collaboration may create long-term professional opportunities.

## Connection to the First Assignment

The first assignment asks interns to identify marketing channels that FlyRank is not currently using but where its target audience is active. The task requires researching real channels, evaluating their audience fit, cost, effort, and potential, and proposing a small test for each recommendation.
